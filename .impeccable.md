## Design Context

### Users

| User | Context | Job to be done |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **Owner / Admin** | Desktop, usually morning planning or end-of-day review | See what's happening across all properties, catch problems early, schedule and pay out |
| **Supervisor** | Tablet or desktop, between properties | Review completed jobs, approve or flag re-cleans |
| **Cleaner** | Mobile (iPhone/Android), on-site | Know exactly what to do, check off tasks, upload proof photos |

**Emotional context**: Admins feel anxious when jobs are untracked or inspections pile up. The app should replace that anxiety with a sense of control — "I see everything, I'm on top of it."

---

### Brand Personality

**Three words**: Calm. Trustworthy. Warm.

- **Calm** — nothing should feel urgent unless something actually is urgent. Whitespace is not wasted space.
- **Trustworthy** — data reads accurately at a glance. Numbers are prominent. Status is never ambiguous.
- **Warm** — the earthy palette (forest green, cream, warm tans) stays. This isn't a cold enterprise tool.

---

### Aesthetic Direction

**Primary references**:
- **Airbnb.com** — rounded corners, warm photography-adjacent colors, approachable and consumer-grade. Borrowed: warmth, rounded cards, generous spacing.
- **Stripe Dashboard** — dense but readable data, excellent typographic hierarchy, strong trust signals. Borrowed: number prominence, clear status chips, reliable layout rhythm.

**Anti-reference**: Not a cold, gray enterprise SaaS. Not cluttered. Not startup-playful.

**Theme**: Light mode only. The warm cream background is a competitive differentiator — keep it.

**Typography**: System fonts (SF Pro / Segoe UI stack). Fast, familiar, zero layout shift.

---

### Design Principles

1. **Calm clarity first** — every screen should communicate "things are under control." Generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, no visual noise.

2. **Numbers read in under a second** — KPIs, counts, and statuses are always prominent. Use large tabular numerals. Status chips are color-coded and bordered (never color-only).

3. **Warm but precise** — the earthy palette is not decoration; it's the brand. All interactive states (hover, focus, active) should feel smooth and intentional, using the existing green/cream tokens.

4. **Speed over decoration** — transitions max 200ms. No gratuitous animation. Skeleton states over blank screens. Perceived performance matters as much as real performance.

5. **Mobile-first execution, desktop-first management** — admin dashboard is information-dense for desktop; cleaner job views are large-target, single-focus, thumb-friendly for mobile.

### Component Conventions

- **Cards**: `rounded-2xl border border-border/70 bg-card shadow-sm` — consistent across all list items and panels
- **Section containers**: `rounded-[1.75rem]` — larger radius for section-level cards
- **Buttons**: `rounded-full` — pill shape throughout
- **Inputs**: `h-12 rounded-xl border border-input bg-background px-4 text-sm`
- **Status chips**: `rounded-full border px-3 py-1 text-xs font-medium` + semantic color pair
- **Page max-width**: `max-w-4xl` (forms/detail) or `max-w-5xl` (dashboard/lists)
- **Sidebar**: `w-60`, `bg-card`, fixed desktop, `border-r border-border/60`
